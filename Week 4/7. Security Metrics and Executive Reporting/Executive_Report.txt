The SOC performance was evaluated using key security metrics including Mean Time to Detect (MTTD), Mean Time to Respond (MTTR), and false positive rate. The current MTTD is 2 hours, indicating efficient threat identification, while the MTTR of 4 hours reflects a moderate response time. The false positive rate of 10% suggests that alert tuning is required to improve detection accuracy and reduce analyst workload.

To enhance SOC efficiency, it is recommended to optimize detection rules, integrate advanced threat intelligence, and automate repetitive response actions. Additionally, continuous monitoring and periodic review of alerts can help reduce dwell time and improve incident response effectiveness. Strengthening user awareness and implementing proactive defense mechanisms will further reduce the likelihood of successful attacks.

Overall, the SOC demonstrates strong detection capabilities but requires improvements in response speed and alert precision.
